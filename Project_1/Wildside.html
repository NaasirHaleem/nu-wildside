Notable Achievements By Northwestern in 2018/2019

Northwestern Football

After a rocky beginning, the Northwestern Football team went 8-1 in the Big Ten and won the West division. They would lose a thrilling game against Ohio State in the Big Ten Championship. However, they rebounded in a major way by upsetting Utah in the Holiday Bowl to win 31-24. They finished 9-5 on the season.

Women's Swimming and Diving

For the second time in two years Olivia Rosendahl took home the gold for the NCAA Championships. She received All-America honors in the 1-meter and Honorable Mention All-America honors in the 3-meter.

Women's Softball

The Northwestern Women's Softball team had a tremendous regular season as they put together a record of 43-10. Unfortunately, they lost in the semi-finals of the Big Ten Tournament against a dominant Minnesota squad. They will be hosting the Evanston regional for the NCAA tournament.

Women's Lacrosse

The Lacrosse team spent the regular season thrashing the competition as they finished the season to the tune of a 15-4 record. That was all put to the test with a monumental showdown against Maryland in the finals of the Big Ten Championship. True to form, the team upset the 'Terps to secure the Big Ten Title. In the first round of the NCAA tournament they downed Notre Dame 13-10. In the second round, the 'Cats claimed revenge on the Syracuse Orange who they had lost to earlier in the season by beating them 18-14.
