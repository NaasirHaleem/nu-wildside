Important Places on Campus

The Northwestern Athletic teams compete at many different locations on campus. Here's our list of them and the best ways to get to them.

Ryan Field

Address: 1501 Central St, Evanston, IL 60208

Teams: Football

Background: Ryan Field is the football stadium of Northwestern University. It's not the most impressive of stadiums but we love it no matter what. It is right next to Welsh Ryan Arena.

Ways To Get There(Note: It is completely possible to walk or bike to either Ryan Field or Welsh-Ryan).:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Purple Line

Welsh-Ryan Arena

Address: 2705 Ashland Ave Evanston, IL 60208

Teams: Men's and Women's Basketball, Wrestling, Women's Volleyball

Background: Welsh-Ryan Arena finished construction in October 2018. It seats 7,309 people. When the place is packed with purple, it really is an incredible experience!

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Purple Line (Central)

Sharon J. Drysdale Field

Address: 1501 Central St. Evanton, IL 60208

Teams: Softball

Background: Sharon J. Drysdale is home to the Northwestern Softball team. It is part of the same campus as Ryan Field. The Softball team won their regional here against Louisville in 2019.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Purple Line (Central)

Rocky and Bernice Miller Park

Address: 2750 Ashland Ave, Evanston, IL 60201

Teams: Baseball

Background: Bernice and Rocky Miller Park finished renovations in 2014. It is located right next to Welsh-Ryan Arena. It was named in honor of Dr. J. Roscoe Miller who was the president of Northwestern from 1949-1970.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Purple Line (Central)

Lanny and Sharon Martin Stadium

Address: 2300 Campus Dr, Evanston, IL 60208

Teams: Soccer, Lacrosse

Background: Home to the world famous Northwestern Women's Lacrosse team and the also spectacular Women's Soccer team, Martin stadium has one of the prettiest backdrops of any college team. It is located right next to Ryan Fieldhouse and the Kellogg Global Hub. It finished construction in 2016.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Walk/Bike

Lakeside Field

Address: 2240 Campus Dr, Evanston, IL 60201

Teams: Field Hockey

Background: Lakeside Field was opened 1997 and renovated in 2015. It is side by side with Martin Stadium.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Walk/Bike

Combe Tennis Center

Address: 2311 Campus Dr, Evanston, IL 60208

Teams: Men's and Women's Tennis

Background: Combe Tennis Center is the home to our wonderful Men's and Women's Tennis teams. It's located within Henry Crown Sports Pavilion (SPAC). It was built in 2001.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Walk/Bike

Norris Aquatics Center

Address: 2311 Campus Dr, Evanston, IL 60208

Teams: Men's and Women's Swim and Dive

Background: The Norris Aquatic Center also resides in SPAC. It is open to Northwestern students and faculty. It consists of two different pools.

Ways To Get There:
1. Intercampus Bus Service
2. Walk/Bike
