Merchandise

Wildside sells merchandise to Northwestern students and Alumni in order to provide Northwestern fans with the option to buy unique, high quality spirit wear. The designs for this merchandise is created by Northwestern students who are either in Wildside or related to it in some manner. In order to ensure that the merchandise that we provide will be what the student body wants, we ask that you vote on our upcoming merchandise. The choice that receives the most votes will be the merchandise that will be sold prior to specific home football games in the fall.
